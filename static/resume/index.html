Summary

I am a programmer and entrepreneur based in Singapore. I co-founded a fintech startup that lets people spend crypto directly using a VISA debit card without having to off-ramp into a bank account, saving users fees and complexity. Before starting my own company, I led engineering at Zilliqa, a high-performance blockchain built from scratch. I also led engineering at Alta Exchange, a regulated private equities exchange in Singapore. I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a Master's in Information Security. However, I prefer to build products rather than break things.
